30-Minute Study Sessions

Focused blocks for consistent progress

The 30-Minute Sweet Spot

Thirty minutes is enough time to make meaningful progress without burning out. It's perfect for commutes, lunch breaks, or focused evening sessions.

30-Minute Session Templates

Template 1: Audio Review Session

Minutes 0-20: Listen to audio overview (1.25-1.5x if review)
Minutes 20-25: Pause, summarize main points out loud
Minutes 25-30: Note any questions or gaps

Best for: Review, commutes, passive study time

Template 2: Active Q&A Session

Minutes 0-5: Review topic briefly
Minutes 5-20: Ask 5-6 questions, read answers carefully
Minutes 20-30: Self-quiz on what you just learned

Best for: New material, conceptual understanding

Template 3: Study Guide Review

Minutes 0-5: Generate or open study guide
Minutes 5-25: Active reading with annotations
Minutes 25-30: Write 3 key takeaways

Best for: Comprehensive topic review

Template 4: Practice Question + Review

Minutes 0-15: Do 5-7 practice questions
Minutes 15-25: Review explanations
Minutes 25-30: Ask NotebookLM about concepts you missed

Best for: Exam prep, identifying gaps

Making the Most of 30 Minutes

Do:

  • Start immediately (no setup time)
  • Focus on one topic or concept
  • End with a summary or takeaway
  • Note what to follow up on later

Don't:

  • Try to cover too much
  • Get distracted by phone/notifications
  • Skip the summary step
  • Leave without knowing next steps
