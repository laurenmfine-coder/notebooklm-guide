What's a Gem?

You've mastered NotebookLM. You're uploading sources, generating Audio Overviews, and getting cited answers. But what if you could customize how NotebookLM talks to you?

Gemini Gems are custom AI assistants with saved instructions. Think of them as AI personas you design once and use forever. And as of December 2025, you can attach your NotebookLM notebooks directly to them.

The Game-Changer

NotebookLM gives you grounded answers from your materials. Gems give you custom behavior. Together? A personalized AI tutor that knows YOUR content and behaves exactly how you want.

Quick Start

Want to try it right now? Here's the fastest path:

  1. Go to gemini.google.com
  2. Click Gem manager → New Gem
  3. Paste this instruction: "You are a Socratic tutor. Never give me answers directly. Instead, ask probing questions that guide me to discover the answer. Reference my attached NotebookLM materials."
  4. Save, then click the paperclip icon → NotebookLM → choose a notebook
  5. Start chatting!
