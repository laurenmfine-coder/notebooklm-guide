Most Popular Prompts

Comparison Table

Works for any topic where you need to compare similar items.

Create a detailed comparison table for [list 3-5 related conditions/medications]. Include columns for: mechanism, presentation, key diagnostic findings, treatment, and important side effects/complications.

Clinical Case

Generate realistic cases to work through for any condition.

Create a realistic clinical case involving [condition]. Include patient demographics, presenting complaint, history, physical exam findings, and relevant labs. Ask me to work through diagnosis and management.

Comprehensive Study Guide

Get a complete overview of any topic.

Create a comprehensive study guide for [topic] that includes: key definitions, pathophysiology, clinical presentation, diagnosis, and management. Organize it in a logical flow.

Practice Questions

Test your knowledge with board-style questions.

Generate 10 multiple-choice questions about [topic] at the level of [Step 1/Step 2/shelf exam]. Include the correct answer and brief explanations for each.

Quick Review

Get the highlights when time is short.

What are the top 10 most high-yield facts about [topic] for [specific exam]? Format as concise bullet points I can review quickly.
