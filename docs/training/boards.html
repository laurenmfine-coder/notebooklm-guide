Board Exam Preparation

High-yield strategies for Step 1, Step 2 CK, and beyond

The Board Prep Challenge

Board exams require integrating massive amounts of information and applying it to clinical scenarios. NotebookLM helps you consolidate knowledge, identify weak areas, and review efficiently.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important

NotebookLM supplements but doesn't replace dedicated board prep resources (First Aid, UWorld, Pathoma, etc.). Use it to enhance those resources, not substitute for them.

Step 1 Strategy

Building Your Master Notebooks

Create comprehensive notebooks by system:

  • Cardiovascular
  • Respiratory
  • Renal
  • GI
  • Endocrine
  • Reproductive
  • Neurology
  • MSK
  • Heme/Onc
  • Immunology
  • Microbiology
  • Pharmacology
  • Biochemistry
  • Behavioral Science

What to Upload

  • Your annotated First Aid pages (photos/scans)
  • Notes from Pathoma, Sketchy, B&B
  • Incorrects from UWorld with explanations
  • High-yield review articles

Audio Strategy

  1. Generate audio overviews for each system (comprehensive review)
  2. Create "weak spot" audio for topics you consistently miss
  3. Build a "high-yield facts" audio playlist for final week
  4. Listen during all available passive time

Step 2 CK Strategy

Clinical Focus

Step 2 CK tests clinical management. Structure your notebooks around:

  • Chief complaints and differentials
  • Diagnostic algorithms
  • Management protocols
  • Patient safety and quality

Leveraging Clinical Experience

  • Your rotation case libraries become review material
  • Generate comprehensive study guides from clinical notes
  • Create audio reviews of high-yield clinical scenarios

Question Bank Integration

The Incorrects Notebook

Create a dedicated notebook for missed questions:

  1. After each question block, copy explanations for missed questions
  2. Add to your incorrects notebook
  3. Generate weekly audio overview of your mistakes
  4. Use Q&A to quiz yourself on previous errors

Pattern Recognition

Ask NotebookLM to identify patterns:

"Based on these missed questions, what topics am I struggling with most? What patterns do you see in my errors?"

Final Week Strategy

Days 7-4 Before Exam

  • Listen to audio overviews of each system
  • Review weak spot notebooks
  • Light question practice

Days 3-1 Before Exam

  • High-yield audio only
  • No new content
  • Review incorrect patterns
  • Rest and mental preparation

Tracking Progress

Use NotebookLM to maintain a study log:

  • Upload weekly QBank performance
  • Generate trend analysis
  • Identify improving vs. persistent weak areas
  • Adjust study plan based on data
