By Time Available

Match your study approach to the time you have

Whether you have 5 minutes between classes or a full study day, here are strategies to make the most of your time with NotebookLM.

  • Maximize short study windows
  • Structure longer study sessions effectively
  • Know what's realistic for each time block

Choose Your Time Block

5 Minutes

Quick wins: single concepts, rapid review, 3-question quizzes.

30 Minutes

Focused study: topic review, clinical cases, practice questions.

1 Hour

Deep learning: comprehensive review, multiple cases, integration.

2-4 Hours

Mastery sessions: organ system review, exam preparation, deep dives.

Quick Reference

Time What's Realistic Best Approach
5 min 1 concept or 3 questions Quick quiz, single comparison, one fact
15 min Focused mini-review 5 questions, brief study guide, 1 case
30 min Complete topic review Audio overview, comprehensive notes, 3 cases
1 hour Deep understanding Full study package, integration, 10+ questions
2-4 hours Topic mastery System review, multiple topics, practice exam
Quality Over Quantity

A focused 15-minute session with active engagement (practice questions, working through cases) beats an hour of passive reading. Match your prompts to encourage active learning regardless of time available.
