Basic Prompts - Cases and Practice

Standard Clinical Case

Best for: Applying knowledge, clinical reasoning practice

Create a realistic clinical case involving [condition]. Include patient demographics, presenting complaint, history, physical exam findings, and relevant labs. Ask me to work through diagnosis and management.

Case Series

Best for: Pattern recognition, understanding varied presentations

Generate 3 cases involving different presentations of [condition]. Show how the same disease can present differently in different patients.

Diagnostic Challenge

Best for: Differential diagnosis practice, avoiding common pitfalls

Create a challenging diagnostic case where [condition] might be missed or confused with other conditions. Include red herrings and subtle clues.

Procedure Practice

Best for: Skills preparation, mental rehearsal

Create a step-by-step checklist for [procedure]. Include preparation, steps in order, safety considerations, and post-procedure care.

Advanced Prompts - Active Learning

Evolving Case

Best for: Dynamic clinical reasoning, adapting to changes

Create a case that evolves over time. Start with initial presentation, then show how the patient's condition changes. Include complications or unexpected findings that require me to adapt my approach.

Critical Decision Point

Best for: Emergency scenarios, time-sensitive decisions

Create a case focused on a critical decision point in [condition/emergency]. Include time pressure, incomplete information, and need to prioritize actions.

Error Analysis Case

Best for: Learning from mistakes, understanding pitfalls

Generate a case where common mistakes might be made in managing [condition]. After I work through it, point out potential pitfalls and how to avoid them.

Hands-On Clinical Skills

Best for: Physical exam technique, clinical skills practice

Describe how to perform [physical exam maneuver] step-by-step. Include patient positioning, what I should do, what I'm looking for, and how to interpret findings.

Differential Diagnosis Practice

DDx Generator

Generate a comprehensive differential diagnosis for a patient presenting with [symptom]. Organize by most common, most serious, and don't-miss diagnoses. Include key distinguishing features.

Narrowing the DDx

Create a case presenting with [symptom]. Then walk me through how to systematically narrow the differential using history, exam, and testing.

Simulation Scenarios

Code Scenario

Create a code/resuscitation scenario involving [situation]. Include vital signs, rhythm, and patient response as I work through ACLS algorithms. Tell me outcomes based on my decisions.

Rapid Response

Create a rapid response scenario where a floor patient is decompensating. Include initial presentation and updates as I make decisions. Challenge me to prioritize interventions.

Handoff Practice

Generate a patient handoff scenario. Give me information about [type of patient] and ask me to present using SBAR format. Then critique my presentation.
Pro Tip

Ask NotebookLM to present cases WITHOUT the answer. Write down your differential and plan before asking for the diagnosis. This active engagement is far more effective than passively reading through cases with answers provided.
