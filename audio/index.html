Essential Prompts for Audio Learners

General Topic Overview

Create an audio overview explaining [topic] in a conversational style, as if two medical students are discussing it over coffee. Focus on the key concepts I need to understand for my exam.

Quick Review Audio

Create a 10-15 minute audio overview covering the highest-yield points about [topic] for quick review before my exam.

Case-Based Discussion

Generate an audio overview that walks through clinical cases involving [condition]. Include how to approach diagnosis and management, with explanations of the reasoning.

Compare and Contrast

Create an audio overview that compares and contrasts [condition A] and [condition B]. Focus on how to differentiate them clinically, including presentation, diagnostic findings, and management differences.

Common Questions

How long are the audio overviews?
Audio overviews typically run 10-20 minutes depending on your source material. More sources and more complex topics tend to generate longer audio.
Can I control what topics are covered in the audio?
Yes! Use the chat feature to guide the focus before generating audio. Ask specific questions or request emphasis on certain topics, then generate the audio.
Can I listen at different speeds?
NotebookLM allows playback speed adjustment. You can also download the audio and use your preferred podcast app for more control.
What if the audio is too basic or too advanced?
Upload different source materials to adjust the level. More advanced textbook chapters create more advanced audio. Lecture slides aimed at your level will match your needs.
