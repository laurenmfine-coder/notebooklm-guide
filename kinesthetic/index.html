Essential Prompts for Kinesthetic Learners

Standard Clinical Case

Create a realistic clinical case involving [condition]. Include patient demographics, presenting complaint, history, physical exam findings, and relevant labs. Ask me to work through diagnosis and management.

Evolving Case

Create a case that evolves over time. Start with initial presentation, then show how the patient's condition changes. Include complications or unexpected findings that require me to adapt my approach.

Diagnostic Challenge

Create a challenging diagnostic case where [condition] might be missed or confused with other conditions. Include red herrings and subtle clues.

Procedure Practice

Create a step-by-step checklist for [procedure]. Include preparation, steps in order, safety considerations, and post-procedure care.

Common Questions

How do I avoid just reading the answer right away?
Ask NotebookLM to present the case first WITHOUT the answer. Write down your differential and plan before asking for the diagnosis. You can say: "Don't tell me the answer yet‚Äîjust present the case."
Can I get multiple cases on the same topic?
Yes! Ask for "3 cases involving different presentations of [condition]" to see how the same disease can present differently. This builds pattern recognition.
What if I get the case wrong?
That's valuable learning! Ask NotebookLM to explain what you missed and why. Request: "Explain why my diagnosis of X was incorrect and what findings should have pointed me to Y."
How can I practice procedures with NotebookLM?
Generate step-by-step checklists, then physically walk through the motions while verbalizing each step. Combine with simulation lab time when available. The mental rehearsal complements hands-on practice.
